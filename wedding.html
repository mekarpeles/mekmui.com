Allergies

Our wedding dinner will be mostly vegan, with gluten-free options available. Please note: while some dishes are naturally gluten-free, they may be prepared in the same oil as gluten-containing items. The kitchen is not a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, so cross-contamination is possible. If this could be an issue for you, please let us know so we can do our best to accommodate your needs.

Please also add your allergies, along with a more detailed description of their severity, in this linked sheet so we can plan accordingly.
